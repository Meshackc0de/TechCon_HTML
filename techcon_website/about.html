About TechCon 2024

Our History

TechCon began in 2015 as a small developer meetup and has since grown into one of the most anticipated technology conferences globally. Over the years, we've hosted over 20,000 attendees across 5 continents, showcasing cutting-edge advancements and real-world tech solutions.

Our Mission

Our mission is to bring together the brightest minds in technology to share knowledge, spark innovation, and inspire the next generation of tech leaders. TechCon is committed to inclusivity, sustainability, and future-forward thinking.
